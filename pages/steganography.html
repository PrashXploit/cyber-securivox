About Steganography

            

                

                    
What is Steganography?

                    

                        Steganography is the practice of concealing information within other non-secret data or physical media. 
                        Unlike cryptography, which scrambles data, steganography hides the very existence of the message.
                    

                    
How it Works

                    

                        This tool uses LSB (Least Significant Bit) steganography to hide text messages in the least significant 
                        bits of image pixels, making the changes virtually invisible to the human eye.
